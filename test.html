Why Registration Matters

Choosing the correct legal structure is one of the most important decisions when starting a business.

Registration gives legal identity, regulatory recognition, and credibility in the market.

We assist in Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Pvt Ltd, OPC, Trust, Society, NGOs.

Business Structures

1

Proprietorship Firm

Simplest business owned by a single individual. No separate legal identity.

  • Min/Max: 1 Owner
  • Time: 3–7 days
  • Documents: PAN, Aadhaar, Address Proof
2

Partnership Firm

Two or more persons sharing profits governed by Partnership Act.

  • Min: 2 | Max: 50
  • Time: 5–10 days
  • Key: Partnership Deed
3

LLP

Combines partnership flexibility with limited liability.

  • Min: 2 Partners
  • Time: 10–15 days
  • Key: LLP Agreement
4

Private Limited Company

Separate legal entity ideal for startups and investors.

  • Directors: 2–15
  • Shareholders: 2–200
  • Time: 10–20 days
5

One Person Company

Single entrepreneur with limited liability.

  • Members: 1
  • Time: 10–15 days
6

Trust / Society

For charitable, social and NGO purposes.

  • Trustees: 2+
  • Members: 7+
  • Time: 7–25 days
