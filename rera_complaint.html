Rera Complaints

RERA complaints help homebuyers protect their rights against delays, fraud, and unfair practices by builders or developers. Under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, buyers can file complaints for issues like project delays, false promises, poor construction quality, and non-compliance with agreed terms.

In today’s real estate environment, it is essential for homebuyers and investors to be aware of their legal rights and take timely action against any malpractice. At Juris Solution, we assist homebuyers, investors, and property owners in filing RERA complaints, ensuring proper documentation, and taking legal action to resolve disputes effectively.

Without filing a proper complaint under RERA, it becomes difficult to claim compensation or hold developers accountable.

Key Benefits of RERA Complaints

Ensures timely possession or compensation for project delays

Protects homebuyers from fraud and false promises by builders

Provides legal support against unfair trade practices

Helps in recovering invested money or claiming refunds

Holds developers accountable under the RERA Act
